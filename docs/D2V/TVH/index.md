# Dealer to Vendor - TVH

[TVH](https://www.tvh.com) (Thermote & Vanhalst) is a Belgian company founded in 1969 and headquartered in Waregem, Belgium, employing more than 5,000 people around the world. It is one of the world's largest suppliers of spare parts and accessories for industrial and agricultural equipment, including forklifts, aerial work platforms, and material handling machinery. With a catalogue spanning millions of parts and a global presence across more than 160 countries, TVH serves dealers and fleet operators who need fast, reliable access to parts sourcing.

The Dysel D2V integration for TVH enables you to automate your pricing, ordering, and logistics, allowing you to work faster and more reliably.
