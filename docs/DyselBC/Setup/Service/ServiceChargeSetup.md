# **User Guide for Setting Up Charges**

This guide will help you add and configure Document Charges for Work Order Quotes and Work Orders in your system. Follow the
steps below to complete the setup process.

* * *

## **Step 1: Navigate to the Charges List**

  1. Access the **Charges** section by using the search feature or selecting an option from the main menu.

* * *

## **Step 2: Create a New Charge**

  1. Click on the **New** button.
  2. A page will open allowing you to set up a new charge.

* * *

## **Step 3: Fill Out the Charge Details**

  1. **Enter the Charge Code:**
  2. Click in the field labeled **Code**. 
  3. Type in the name for your charge (e.g., Service Charge).

  4. **Enter the Description:**

  5. Click in the field labeled **Description**. 
  6. Type a short description (e.g., Service Charge).

  7. **Select the Charge Type:**

  8. Click in the field labeled **Charge Type**. 
  9. Enter or select a relevant value (e.g., 1 for a specific type of charge).

* * *

## **Step 4: Configure Posting Group and Tax Details**

### **Set Up the Posting Group**

  1. Click in the field labeled **Gen. Prod. Posting Group**. 
  2. A list will appear; choose the appropriate option by clicking on it. 
  3. Click **OK** to confirm your choice.

### **Set Up the Tax Group Code**

  1. Click in the field labeled **Tax Group Code**. 
  2. A list will appear; choose the appropriate option by clicking on it. 
  3. Click **OK** to confirm your choice.

* * *

## **Step 5: Customize Document Charge Settings**

### **Modify Document Type Filters**

  1. Click on the field labeled **On Document Type**. 
  2. Select the desired type (e.g., 1 for specific documents).

### **Set Up Line Type Filters**

  1. Click on the field labeled **Line Type Filter**. 
  2. When the **Line Type Filter** page opens: 
  3. Use the checkboxes to select the options that apply. 
  4. Click **OK** to confirm your selections.

### **Set Up Service Type Filters**

  1. Click on **Service Type Filter**. 
  2. In the **Service Type Filter** page: 
  3. Use the checkboxes to select the appropriate service types. 
  4. Click **OK** to finalize your choices.

### **Set Up Customer Price Group Filters**

  1. Click on **Customer Price Group Filter**. 
  2. In the **Customer Price Group Filter** page: 
  3. Use the checkboxes to select the appropriate service types. 
  4. Click **OK** to finalize your choices.

* * *

## **Step 6: Configure Fee and Amounts**

  1. **Set the Fee Percentage:**
  2. Click on the field labeled **Fee %**. 
  3. Enter the percentage value (e.g., 10).
  4. **Enter the Minimum Amount:**
  5. Click on **Minimum Amount (LCY)**. 
  6. Enter the minimum amount for this charge (e.g., 1).
  7. **Enter the Maximum Amount:**
  8. Click on **Maximum Amount (LCY)**. 
  9. Enter the maximum amount (e.g., 250).

**NOTE** If a Charge needs to be a flat rate, enter a percentage of 1% and the same amount for both the Minimum and Maximum Amounts.

* * *

## **Step 7: Configure Equipment Category**

  1. Click on the field labeled **Equipment Category**.
  2. Select the appropriate category or value.

* * *

## **Final Step: Review and Save**

  1. Double-check all configured fields to ensure accuracy.
  2. Click **Save** to finish setting up your charge.

* * *

Congratulations! You have successfully added and configured charges.
