# **User Instructions for Converting Maintenance Quote to a Maintenance Contract

This guide walks you through the steps to convert a maintenance quote into a
maintenance contract in the system.

* * *

### **Step 1: Open the Maintenance Contract Quotes Page**

  1. Start by navigating to the **Maintenance Contract Quotes** section. 
  2. You can find this page by either selecting it from the menu or using the search bar and entering “Maintenance Contract Quotes”. 

* * *

### **Step 2: Select a Maintenance Quote**

  1. Once you see the list of maintenance quotes, find the specific quote that you want to convert into a contract. 
  2. Click on the row for the maintenance quote to select it.

* * *

### **Step 3: Edit the Maintenance Quote**

  1. With the quote selected, click the **Edit** button. This will open the details of the selected maintenance quote.

* * *

### **Step 4: Create a Maintenance Contract**

  1. Once the maintenance quote details are visible, look for the option to **Create Contract** and click on it.

* * *

### **Step 5: Confirm the Action**

  1. A confirmation box will appear asking if you’re sure you want to create a contract. Click **Yes** to proceed. 
  2. You might see another confirmation box. If it appears, click **Yes** again to complete the action. 

* * *

### **Step 6: Review the Maintenance Contract**

  1. After confirming, the system will automatically open the newly created **Maintenance Contract** for you to review its details. 
  2. Once you are done reviewing, close the contract page.

* * *

### **Step 7: Close the Maintenance Quote Page**

  1. After closing the contract, you’ll return to the **Maintenance Quote** page. Be sure to close this page as well to complete the process. 

* * *

You have successfully converted a maintenance quote to a maintenance contract!
