# Rental
The Rental module manages the full lifecycle of equipment rentals, from quoting and contract management through to invoicing and return. Contracts can be built from templates and enriched with rental phases, transport lines, overtime periods, and object-level configurations. Availability forecasting and reservation tools help ensure the right equipment is allocated to the right contract at the right time. Invoicing supports consolidated billing periods and integrates with the standard Business Central sales flow for a seamless financial process.
