# Dysel Projects
Dysel Projects gives your organization a single, structured place to manage complex, multi-stage work engagements from first enquiry through to final invoice. Whether you are building new equipment, executing a major conversion, or delivering a long-running service engagement, Dysel Projects keeps costs, planning, and billing aligned in one connected record.

From the project card you coordinate everything: define the tasks that make up the work, set budgets for labour, parts, and third-party costs, create and track work orders, manage purchase requests, and control exactly when and how the customer is invoiced. Progress is always visible — actual costs flow in automatically from work orders, time sheets, purchase invoices, and rental contracts, giving you a live view of profitability at any point in the project's life.

Billing is flexible by design. Each task can be invoiced on a fixed schedule, based on actual costs, as a percentage of budget, or against completion milestones — or any combination of these. WIP accounting ensures that revenue and costs are recognized correctly in your financial statements even when billing and delivery are not perfectly synchronized.

Dysel Projects is built on Dysel W1 and integrates natively with Work Order Management, Equipment Management, Rental Management, Time Sheets, and Consolidated Orders.
